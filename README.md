## **📖Introduction**

Welcome to _Magic Worlds_ where everything is possible and the only limit is your imagination and dreams. Forever free, family friendly and open source.

_Cars World_ is the ultimate virtual paradise for car enthusiasts. Test your driving skills, race against AI and real players, customize vehicles, and explore stunning automotive landscapes. Whether you're into high-speed racing, off-road challenges, or building your dream car, _Cars World_ offers endless possibilities for adrenaline-fueled fun!

## **🚀How to Access Cars World**

1. **Launch Magic Worlds** and enter your spaceship.
2. **Talk to the Vice Captain** by walking up ahead and pressing `E`.
3. **Head straight up to the bridge** and enter by pressing `E`.
4. **Once in the bridge, climb up the platform** to access the travel console.
5. **Access the console** by entering the passcode `[789]`.
6. **Select Cars World** in the World Hub.
7. **Start Exploring**: Compete in races, unlock new vehicles, and customize your dream ride.

## **🎮Gameplay & Features**

- 🚗 **Street Racing** – Burn rubber in high-speed street races and tournaments.
- 🏆 **Off-Road Challenges** – Conquer extreme terrain and test your driving skills.
- 🏎️ **Car Customization** – Modify, paint, and upgrade your vehicle.
- ⚙️ **Realistic Driving Physics** – Experience true-to-life handling and mechanics.
- 🪕 **Classic & Future Cars** – Drive legendary classics and futuristic concept cars.
- 🏁 **Multiplayer Mode** – Challenge your friends and climb the leaderboard.
- 🎤 **Engine Sounds & Effects** – Enjoy realistic revs, drifts, and tire squeals.
- 🛠️ **Unlockable Rewards** – Earn special cars, accessories, and trophies.

## **⚙️Technical Details**

### **🚨System Requirements**

#### Minimum:

- **OS**: Windows 10 (64-bit) / Linux / macOS 10.15+
- **Processor**: Intel Core i5-4460 / AMD FX-6300
- **Memory**: 8 GB RAM
- **Graphics**: NVIDIA GTX 760 / AMD Radeon R7 260X
- **DirectX**: Version 11
- **Storage**: 50 GB available space

#### Recommended:

- **OS**: Windows 11 / Latest macOS / Linux (Latest)
- **Processor**: Intel Core i7-8700 / AMD Ryzen 5 3600
- **Memory**: 16 GB RAM
- **Graphics**: NVIDIA RTX 2060 / AMD RX 5700 XT

- **Storage**: 50 GB SSD
